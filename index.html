Frequently Asked Questions

Is SnapText really free?+

Yes, completely free. No trials, no premium tier, no hidden costs. The extension is open source — you can inspect every line of code on GitHub.

Do I need an account?+

No. SnapText works fully offline with local storage. You only need a free account if you want to back up your macros to the cloud or sync them across multiple devices.

Does it work on browsers other than Chrome?+

Yes — SnapText works on any Chromium-based browser including Chrome, Brave, Edge, Arc, Opera, and Vivaldi. Load it the same way via the extensions page.

Is my data private?+

Your macros are stored locally in your browser by default. If you enable cloud sync, data is stored in Firebase with security rules that ensure only you can access your own macros. No analytics, no tracking, no ads.

What if I uninstall the extension — will I lose my macros?+

If you're using local-only storage, yes — uninstalling or clearing browser data removes your macros. To protect against this, either export your macros as a JSON/CSV file, or create a free account to back them up to the cloud.

Can I share macros with my team?+

Yes. Sign in, go to the Share & Explore tab in the dashboard, and publish a macro pack. You'll get an 8-character code anyone can use to import your macros. You can also make packs public for the Explore library.
