Customer Support

Support teams handle hundreds of tickets daily. SnapText lets agents respond instantly with professional, consistent messages while personalizing each response with dynamic variables.

;ack
Acknowledge a support ticket
Hi {{input:Customer Name}}, Thank you for reaching out to support. I've received your request and will look into this right away. I'll follow up with an update within {{select:1 hour,2 hours,24 hours}}. Best regards, {{input:Your Name}}
;refund
Process a refund request
Hi {{input:Customer Name}}, I've processed your refund request. You should see the amount credited back to your account within 5-7 business days. Reference number: {{input:Ref #}} Is there anything else I can help you with? Best, {{input:Your Name}}
;escalate
Escalate to senior support
Hi {{input:Customer Name}}, I've escalated your case to our senior support team for further review. A specialist will reach out to you within 24 hours. Your escalation reference: ESC-{{date}}-{{random:1234,5678,9012}} Thank you for your patience.

Sales Teams

Close more deals faster with instant outreach templates. Personalize every message while maintaining consistent messaging across your entire team.

;outreach
Initial cold outreach email
Hi {{input:First Name}}, I noticed that {{input:Company}} is {{input:recent achievement/news}}. Congratulations! I'm reaching out because companies like yours often struggle with {{input:pain point}}, and I thought you'd be interested in how we've helped similar businesses achieve {{input:result}}. Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call this week? Best, {{input:Your Name}}
;followup
Follow-up after no response
Hi {{input:First Name}}, I wanted to follow up on my previous email about {{input:topic}}. I understand you're busy, so I'll keep this brief. {{select:Would a different time work better for a call?,Would it help if I sent over some case studies?,Should I connect with someone else on your team?}} Either way, I'd love to hear from you. Best, {{input:Your Name}}
;proposal
Send proposal follow-up
Hi {{input:First Name}}, Thank you for the great conversation on {{input:meeting date}}. As promised, I've attached our proposal for {{input:Company}}. Highlights: - {{input:Key benefit 1}} - {{input:Key benefit 2}} - Investment: {{input:Price}} I'm available to discuss any questions. When would be a good time for a follow-up call? Best, {{input:Your Name}}
